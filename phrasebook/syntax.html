Phrases are written like this: get-[noun]-*, where fragments are written straightforwardly: get, tags are written in brackets: [noun], and the * symbol indicates that the phrase can be expanded.

If concrete fragments matching a given tag precedes the tag, the tag is marked with a slash. For instance, the phrase book contains the phrases is-empty-*, is-valid-* and is-[/adjective]-*, where [/adjective] signifies adjectives except "empty" and "valid".
